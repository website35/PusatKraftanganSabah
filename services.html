Our Craftsmanship

Handicraft Production

Our artisans meticulously create traditional textiles, batik, and rattan works that carry the soul of Sabah.

Training & Workshops

We provide hands-on experience for those wishing to learn the ancient arts of weaving and painting.

Handicraft Gallery

Our gallery serves as a living museum, showcasing the evolution of Sabahan crafts. From ancient tribal motifs to modern adaptations, it is a space where history meets contemporary design.

Handicraft Arts

Handicraft is more than just making items; it is a form of fine art. Every stroke of batik wax and every strand of rattan weave tells a story of the artisan’s dedication and skill.

Handicrafts Beauty

The true beauty of a handmade item lies in its imperfections and uniqueness. No two pieces are exactly alike, reflecting the natural textures of Sabah's raw materials and the human touch.
